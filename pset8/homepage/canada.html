My trip to Canada

I went to Canada in 2019 to visit my sister. Spent around 20 days there, specially in Vancouver, the city they live in.
During this time, I visited several places a few of which I'll share in here. First, as usual, let me introduce everybody:

That's me, my sister and my brother-in-law. My mother is also there with us (she's the one taking the picture), but I don't feel comfortable sharing her pictures.

Grouse Mountain

While my mother and my sister spent the day together, I went to visit the Grouse Moutains.
I originally planned to hike the trail, but due to the cold, I tooke the Grouse Grind instead.
Beautiful place, where you can have a nice view of all Vancouver.
It is a shame I didn't know how to ski, because I would love to spend more time in there.

Stanley Park

A very good vibe. Excellent place to jog and walk with the family.
When I took those pictures I was alone. I wanted to get the sunset and the sunrise.
